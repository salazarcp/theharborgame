Privacy Policy for Harbor

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Castilva Games ("we," "us," or "our") respects the privacy of our players and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit theharborgame.com or play Harbor.

2. Information We Collect

To operate the website and game, provide multiplayer services, maintain game integrity, and save progress, we may process the following data:

  • Account Information: Your Epic Games Account ID to identify you across sessions.
  • Gameplay Data: In-game progress, inventory, survival statistics (health, hunger, etc.), and location data within the game world.
  • Technical Data: IP address, hardware details, and network information required for connection to our game servers and for anti-cheat purposes.
  • Optional Website Analytics: After consent, page views, referral and campaign information, approximate device and browser details, and interactions such as Steam, trailer, Discord, and newsletter clicks.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use this information strictly to:

  • Facilitate multiplayer gameplay and matchmaking.
  • Persist your character's state, inventory, and base building progress.
  • Detect and prevent cheating or unauthorized modifications to ensure a fair environment.
  • Analyze game stability and fix technical issues.
  • Measure which pages and campaigns lead visitors to Harbor's official store and community pages, when analytics consent is granted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to support Harbor and its official website. Depending on the service you use and your consent choice, data may be processed by:

  • Epic Online Services (EOS): For authentication, matchmaking, and player data management.
  • Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC): For analyzing game client integrity to prevent cheating. EAC may collect and process data regarding game binaries and system memory purely for cheat detection.
  • Google Analytics 4: For consent-based website measurement. Advertising storage, advertising personalization, and Google Signals are disabled by our implementation.

5. Website Analytics and Consent

Google Analytics 4 does not load on theharborgame.com unless you select "Accept" in the analytics notice. If you reject optional analytics, the Google Analytics script and analytics cookies are not loaded.

Your choice is stored locally in your browser under harbor_analytics_consent so the site can remember it. You can reopen the notice through "Analytics Settings" in the website footer and change your choice. Revoking consent stops future analytics collection and removes Harbor's Google Analytics cookies that are available to the site.

We use Measurement ID G-XMBR4ENVMK. Google may process technical information to provide aggregated measurement, security, and approximate geographic reporting; individual IP addresses are not exposed to us in Google Analytics reports.

6. Data Retention

We retain gameplay data for as long as you maintain an active account or as required to provide the service. Consent-based website event data follows the retention settings of our Google Analytics 4 property; aggregated reports may be kept longer. You may request information or deletion at any time.
